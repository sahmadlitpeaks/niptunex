What we screen

Three perspectives, one sample

A modern prenatal panel that goes beyond fetal aneuploidies — adding maternal risk markers and personalised micronutrient guidance to support a healthier pregnancy.

Baby · cfDNA / NIPT

Fetal screening

Cell-free DNA analysis identifies the most common trisomies and reports fetal sex from a maternal blood sample.

  • Trisomy 21 (Down syndrome)
  • Trisomy 18 (Edwards)
  • Trisomy 13 (Patau)
  • Fetal sex determination
  • Optional: Sex chromosome aneuploidies (XO, XXY, XYY, XXX)
Mother · Predispositions

Maternal insights

Genetic markers associated with common pregnancy complications — supporting earlier monitoring and proactive care.

  • Gestational diabetes risk
  • Preeclampsia susceptibility
  • Gestational hypertension
  • Recurrent miscarriage markers
  • Thrombophilia risk variants
Nutrition · Micronutrients

Personalised nutrition

Variants that influence the body's processing of key vitamins and minerals — guiding personalised supplementation.

  • Iron metabolism & absorption
  • Vitamin B9 (folate) & B12
  • Vitamin C, D & magnesium
  • Manganese requirements
  • Omega-3 conversion efficiency
